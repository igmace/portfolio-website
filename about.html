About

My Story

Intro: Hello, I’m Iris Mace! I’m originally from the Baltimore-Washington Metro area but I made the move to Phoenix, Arizona with my husband and pets six years ago. While looking for my place in the design world, I stumbled across UI/UX design and fell in love. The idea of design with a purpose and impact really appealed to me. I like to think of myself as an advocate for the user. I love conducting user interviews to learn the users’ challenges and aspirations. I do my best to implement great user-centered designs by communicating well with everyone involved in the design process, including my engineering team.

When I’m not designing, I like to spend quality time with my husband and pets. I love to travel whenever I get the chance – even if it’s just a day trip. I have many interests – most of which are creative like drawing, building in the Sims 4 or pretending I’m an interior designer in my own home. I also love exploring new languages through music or study.
